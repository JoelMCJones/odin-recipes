Cheesy Hamburger Rice Casserole

Quick Fix Delight: 5-Ingredient Cheesy Rice Bliss!

Cook up a storm without breaking a sweat with this five-ingredient wonder, a rice casserole that's pure comfort in every bite. Topped with melty mozzarella for an easy-breezy remix of grandma's classic. Simple, scrumptious, and ready in a snap

Ingredients Needed

  • 500 Grams of ground beef
  • 1 (300G) can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 (410G) can diced tomatoes with juice
  • ½ cup long grain white rice
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste

5 Easy Steps

  1. Step one - Get down with the ground beef in a skillet dance-off over medium-high heat. Shake and stir until it's groovin' in evenly browned perfection.
  2. Step two - Drain the grease and join the party by adding cream of mushroom soup, tomatoes, and uncooked rice. It's a saucy, soupy shindig.
  3. Step three - Throw a lid on the pan, let the flavors jam over low heat. Stir occasionally and let the rice rock and roll for about 15 minutes until it's cooked and ready to party.
  4. Step four - Crank up the heat, it's time for the oven's broiler to shine. Transfer the skillet sensation to a casserole dish. Cover it up with a cheesy blanket, creating a layer that's about to melt hearts.
  5. Step five - Broil until the cheese is all melty and toasty. Season with a dash of salt and pepper, tune in to taste, and get ready to enjoy the flavorful groove!
