Chocolate Peanut Butter Protein Bars

Chocolate peanut butter protein bars are easy to make in a small batch, and absolutely delicious

Using a good protein powder is the key. If there is a downside, it might be how hard it will be to only eat one.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup vanilla protein powder
  • 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil

7 Easy Steps

  1. Panorama of Parchment Prestige: Embrace an 8.5x4.5-inch loaf pan and drape it in parchment paper. the red carpet for your soon-to-be snack stars. Allow for an overhang; it's the grand entrance they deserve. Reserve this theatrical setup for the snack show.
  2. Protein Alchemy Extravaganza: In a bowl, toss together peanut butter, protein powder, maple syrup, vanilla, and a pinch of salt. Mix it like you're composing a snack symphony. Press this celestial blend into the pan. The flavor curtain rises!
  3. Choco Chip Conclave: Summon chocolate chips and oil to a microwave-safe bowl. the secret society of sweet indulgence.
  4. Microwave Minuet: Heat things up for 30 seconds, a quick dance. Stir like you're choreographing a cocoa ballet. Repeat until those chips surrender and become a smooth, delectable harmony.
  5. Choco Cascade Enchantment: Pour that molten chocolate cascade over your peanut butter opus. Smooth it out like a chocolatier sculpting a masterpiece.
  6. Fridge Zen Retreat: Allow your creation a spa day in the fridge. Wait patiently (or not) for about 30 minutes. their meditation time until they're set and snack nirvana is achieved.
  7. Baroque Ensemble Premiere: It's a grand unveiling! Cut your creation into 8 bars. These aren't just bars; they're culinary performers ready for their grand debut. Enjoy the epicurean spectacle!
